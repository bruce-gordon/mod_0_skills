Class: Menu

Menu Attributes:
entreeTitles (array)
totalMenuItems (integer)
itemsPreviouslyOrdered (integer)
isOpen (boolean)
color (string)

Menu Methods:
addEntree (adds 1 additional entree string to the entree array)
itemsUnordered (totalMenuItems - itemsPreviouslyOrdered)
closeMenu (changes isOpen to false)
changeColor (changes color)
